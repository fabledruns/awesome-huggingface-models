# Awesome Hugging Face Models

A curated list of the best and most popular models on [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co).  
This repository serves as a quick reference for developers, researchers, and AI enthusiasts who want to explore state-of-the-art models across different domains like NLP, Computer Vision, Speech, and more.

## 📚 Categories

### 🔤 Natural Language Processing
- [bert-base-uncased](https://huggingface.co/bert-base-uncased) – Classic BERT model for general NLP tasks.
- [gpt2](https://huggingface.co/gpt2) – Generative language model for text generation.
- [facebook/bart-large](https://huggingface.co/facebook/bart-large) – Seq2Seq model for summarization and translation.
- [meta-llama/Llama-2-7b](https://huggingface.co/meta-llama/Llama-2-7b) – Open LLaMA model for general AI tasks.

### 🖼️ Computer Vision
- [openai/clip-vit-base-patch32](https://huggingface.co/openai/clip-vit-base-patch32) – Connects text and images.
- [google/vit-base-patch16-224](https://huggingface.co/google/vit-base-patch16-224) – Vision Transformer for image classification.
- [facebook/detectron2](https://huggingface.co/facebook/detectron2) – Object detection and segmentation.

### 🎙️ Speech & Audio
- [facebook/wav2vec2-base-960h](https://huggingface.co/facebook/wav2vec2-base-960h) – Speech recognition.
- [openai/whisper-small](https://huggingface.co/openai/whisper-small) – Multilingual speech-to-text.
- [speechbrain/sepformer-whamr](https://huggingface.co/speechbrain/sepformer-whamr) – Speech separation.

### 🎨 Multimodal / Generative
- [stabilityai/stable-diffusion-2-1](https://huggingface.co/stabilityai/stable-diffusion-2-1) – Image generation.
- [runwayml/stable-diffusion-v1-5](https://huggingface.co/runwayml/stable-diffusion-v1-5) – Popular SD checkpoint.
- [stabilityai/sdxl-turbo](https://huggingface.co/stabilityai/sdxl-turbo) – Fast text-to-image generation.
- [facebook/musicgen-small](https://huggingface.co/facebook/musicgen-small) – AI music generation.
